White Peachy Sangria

A light and refreshing pineapple drink. Beat the heat this summer with this simple drink.

Ingredients:

  • 4 (750 ml) bottles moscato wine
  • 4 (12 oz.) cans or bottles lemon-lime soda (such as Sprite¬Æ)
  • 1 cup brandy
  • 1 cup peach schnapps
  • 1 (16 ounce) package fresh strawberries, sliced
  • 2 fresh peaches, pitted and sliced
  • 2 mangos - peeled, seeded, and sliced
  • ¬Ω fresh pineapple - peeled, cored, and cut into chunks
  • 1 (6 oz) container fresh raspberries

Directions:

  • Stir moscato wine, lemon-lime soda, brandy, peach schnapps, peaches, strawberries, mangos, pineapple chunks, and raspberries together in a large container and chill 8 hours or overnight.
